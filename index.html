THE LEGEND OF ZELDA: BREATH OF THE WILD

Developed and Published by Nintendo

Step into a world of adventure with The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild!

Breath of the Wild is an open world game where you can adventure anywhere and do anything you desire to explore Hyrule. Link's mission is to save Zelda and defeat Ganon, which is explained thorughout the gameplay. There are exciting battles, puzzles galore, and so many fun adventures to have during this game. The scenery is breathtaking and the interactions with every object and person is amazing. The freedom of choices is really amazing and this game will keep you entertained for hundreds of hours. With hundreds of hidden puzzles and minigames and so much more. Breath of the Wild is available on the Nintendo Switch and the Nintendo Wii U.


Gameplay Features

  • Throughout the world there are over one hundred shrines which each contain a unique puzzle to accomplish and rewards along the way. When you complete shrines you are rewarded with more health or stamina, and they provide a teleportation opportunity, which is very helpful in navigating around.

  • Throughout the game there are so many mini puzzles to complete and completing them will give you rewards. They are not part of any mission or objective and a really fun bonus to the game.

  • You can interact with so many objects in the game, from buildings to animals, pots to fire, rocks to grass. You can pick food, ride animals, climb up buildings and rock faces. You can also buy and sell objects which is helpful.

  • Since you can go all around by running, paragliding, swimming, or teleporting you can see some amazing views. You can travel around the map and find towns, stables, monster hideouts, castles, islands, and so much more. There are so many places to go and explore.

Further Game Information

You awake with no kingdom, no memories, and characters who keep recognizing you as a great hero. Play as Link to save the kingdom from the clutches of the evil king Ganon. Link awakes in a world he no longer remembers so he must adventure and travel around to figure out exactly what he needs to do (save the world) and how to do it. This supposed hero must explore and defeat villians and monsters along the way. Treasure is to be found all around and can be sold or used in a variety of ways. With only what you can find (which turns out to be much more than you'd expect) you advance through the game in any order you want.

The game can be played in almost any order that the player wants! Besides the first level, which is just introductory, the player can travel all around and go wherever they want and unlock whatever area they want next! Once you reach certain points in the land (towers and shrines) you can teleport to the various locations to aid in speedy travel!

As you travel across vast fields, through forests, and up mountain peaks, you will discover what has become of the kingdom of Hyrule in this stunning world. With the ability to paraglide, run, ride horses, swim, and climb, you can go just about anywhere you please!

There are hundreds of different puzzles and hundreds of side quests to discover and complete. With this game you will never get bored as there is always something new to do.

Breath of the Wild received critical acclaim for its open-ended gameplay and attention to detail. Critics called it a landmark in open world game design. It has won several game of the year awards and has since been cited as one of the greatest video games of all time. Following are some technical facts if you feel inclined to learn more.

  • It was released on March 3, 2017.
  • By 2021 it had sold nearly 26 million copies, making it one of the best-selling video games of all time.
  • A spin-off, Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity, was released in 2020.
  • A sequel is set for release on the Switch in 2022.

You never know what will happen in the game, so start exploring!
